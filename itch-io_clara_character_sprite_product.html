Clara is a character asset sprite designed primarily for story-rich games, such as visual novels, but can also be used in other media like YouTube videos.

  • Full-body Layered PSD file 
  • Resolution: 750x1500px (FREE) / 1000x2000px (PAID)
  • Distinct layers separating body, clothing, hair, facial expressions, and accessories
  • Toggleable clothes and expressions for flexible design


Includes a highly organized, layered PSD file.

 Control the visibility of individual elements to your project:

  • Clothing: Jacket, tank top, socks, sneakers
  • Accessories: Earrings, necklace
  • Expressions: Eyes (open, closed) and Mouth (open,closed and teeth)


Choose the direction the character is facing. 


Clara's face features 28 toggleable expressions, with variations for open/closed eyes and 3 mouth types (open ,closed and teeth) , allowing for a wide range of emotional displays.

  • Neutral, happy, surprised, confident, stoic, serious, confused, uneasy
  • Crying, sobbing, angry, furious, scared, fright, terror
  • Disgusted, nauseating, tired, sleepy, exhausted
  • Blush, embarrassed, smirk, mock, psychotic, soulless, kiss

Every detail was thoughtfully hand-crafted to show Clara's personality.
